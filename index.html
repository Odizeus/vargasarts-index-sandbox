Artist Statement

Art shaped by imagination, nature and emotion.

Every artwork begins with a single idea — a feeling, a place, a moment of stillness — and slowly becomes a visual story. From wildlife and landscapes to fantasy and abstract worlds, each piece carries a voice of its own.

"I paint what I dream and draw what I feel. The canvas is just where the story lands."

Ulises Vargas Artist · Painter · Storyteller

About

A life built
around making art.

Working from Gainesville, Florida, Ulises Vargas creates original paintings, drawings and digital pieces inspired by wildlife, fantasy and the natural world. His focus stays on emotion, movement, color and storytelling — building images that hold a feeling long after you stop looking.
