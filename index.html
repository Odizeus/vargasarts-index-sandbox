About the Artist

Art shaped by imagination, nature and emotion.

Ulises Vargas creates original paintings, drawings and digital artwork inspired by wildlife, fantasy and the natural world. His work focuses on emotion, movement, color and storytelling.

Every artwork begins with an idea, a feeling or a moment of inspiration transformed into a visual story.
