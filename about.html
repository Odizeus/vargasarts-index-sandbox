About the artist

Ulises Vargas

Visual artist and painter creating original artwork where realism, fantasy, nature and personal imagination meet.

Born in Güines, Cuba • Creating in Gainesville, Florida • Original Paintings, Drawings, Digital Art & Murals

Ulises Vargas

Artist, painter and creator of wildlife, fantasy, landscape, digital and mural artwork.

Early beginning

Born and raised in Güines, Cuba, Ulises Vargas developed a deep passion for visual arts during childhood. By the age of eight, he was already spending countless hours drawing, painting, studying forms, and learning how imagination could become something visible.

Recognition

His dedication brought early recognition in art contests and festivals, including first place and honorable mention in The Golden Age state contest in 1997, first place in a county contest the same year, awards at the Güines Festival and South Melena county contest in 2000, and an honorable mention at the PAEME Festival in 2004.

Life and return to art

In later years, Ulises pursued a career as an Orthopedic and Traumatology Technician, placing his artistic path to the side for a time. The passion never disappeared. He continued painting and drawing whenever possible, and in 2018 he returned to art with renewed purpose.

Artistic vision

Today, his artwork blends observation, emotion, research and imagination. Through wildlife, fantasy, landscapes and symbolic detail, he creates pieces that invite viewers to slow down, look closer, and experience the beauty of the world through his eyes.

“My work is a way to honor beauty, nature, imagination and the personal stories that live behind every brushstroke.”
