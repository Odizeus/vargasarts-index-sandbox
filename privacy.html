Privacy Policy

How Vargas Arts handles contact inquiries, basic technical information, and third-party resources used by this artist portfolio website.

Plain-language notice

This page is practical website information for visitors and is not legal advice.

Last updated: June 19, 2026

About this website

Vargas Arts is an artist portfolio website operated by Ulises Vargas / Vargas Arts. The site presents artwork, artist information, videos, sales information, and ways to contact the artist.

Information you may send

If you contact the artist or send a commission inquiry, Vargas Arts may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, message, project details, budget or timeline information, and any other details included in your inquiry.

The contact page uses a Formspree contact form, so form submissions may be processed by Formspree before they reach Vargas Arts.

Basic technical information

When you visit the site, basic technical information may be processed automatically by hosting providers, CDN services, browser requests, security systems, and similar services needed to display the website. This can include IP address, browser type, device information, pages requested, referring pages, and request times.

Third-party resources

The site may use or link to third-party services and resources, including hosting, fonts, CDN libraries, social media links, embedded or linked video platforms, and external platforms such as Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Google Fonts, Font Awesome, Bootstrap CDN, and Formspree.

Vargas Arts does not intentionally use advertising or analytics cookies, but third-party services or embedded content may process technical information according to their own policies.

Third-party websites and platforms have their own privacy practices. Vargas Arts is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external websites.

How information is used

Information you send may be used to respond to your inquiry, discuss artwork, commissions, murals, availability, pricing, timelines, exhibitions, or collaborations, and maintain reasonable business records related to the conversation.

Vargas Arts does not intentionally sell personal information.

Children

This website is not directed to children under 13. Visitors under 13 should not submit personal information through the website.

Your choices

You may contact the artist to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information that you submitted through the website. Some information may need to be retained when required for legitimate recordkeeping, security, or legal reasons.

To make a request, use the contact page.
